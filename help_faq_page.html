UMBC Dining Help & FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I order online?

You can order online by selecting your dining venue on the homepage, then choosing your items from the menu. Proceed to checkout, customize your items, choose pickup or delivery, and select your payment method.

Can I use my meal plan to pay online?

Yes, you can use your on-campus meal plan to pay for meals online along with other standard payment methods such as credit cards.

What should I do if I encounter an issue with my order?

Please contact the dining venue directly using the contact information provided on our order confirmation page. If further assistance is needed, you can contact UMBC Dining Services.
